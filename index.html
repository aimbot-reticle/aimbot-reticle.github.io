AimBot: A Simple Auxiliary Visual Cue to Enhance Spatial Awareness of Visuomotor Policies

▶ Please wait a few seconds for the videos to load. (Tested on safari and chrome)
▶ 450 videos (5 tasks x 9 policies x 10 episodes) are organized by columns for each policy and rows for each episode.
▶ Each video has a corresponding success/failure with the episode (trial) number.
▶ Press the play button on any video to watch it.
▶ While a video is playing, press the F key to enter fullscreen mode and ESC to exit.
▶ Click "Show More" to reveal additional episodes for each task. ▶ Each episode number starts from the same initial configuration.
